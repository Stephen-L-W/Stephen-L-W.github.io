About Us

We have owned this farm since 1996 and started the petting zoo in 1999.

We started off small with just a few different types of animals and thanks to the support of everyone, we were able to grow and add new animals to our farm.

We make sure to care for the animals on the farm so that they can be happy and make veryone that visits happy as well.

We also grow and harvest our own crops to help feed the animals and to keep them healthy.

We look forward to keeping the family buisness going to continue bringing smiles to everyone that visits.
